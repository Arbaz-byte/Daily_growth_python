# Day 6 – Eigenvalues & Eigenvectors

**Date:** 2026-05-23

## Topics Covered
- Eigenvalues/vectors of a random symmetric matrix (real, orthogonal eigenvectors)
- Verification of `A v = λ v` with tolerance
- Diagonalisation: `P^T A P = D` using orthogonal eigenvectors
- Geometric interpretation: unit circle → ellipse, eigenvectors as principal axes

## Files Created

## Key Learnings
- Symmetric matrices have real eigenvalues and orthonormal eigenvectors
- Spectral theorem: `A = Q D Q^T`
- Diagonalisation simplifies matrix powers and decouples systems
- Eigenvectors align with ellipse axes; eigenvalues determine stretch
